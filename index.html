How I work

STEP 1

Research

Understanding the objective of the
problem by breaking down into sub
problems, Learning about the customers
and trying to find efficient and effective
approach for the solution.
STEP 2

User Journey

Investigate the user journey. Doing quantitative and qualitative research of the product and services to be delivered.Create a number of scenarios and personalities to aid in the decision-making process.
STEP 3

Design Exploration

Following a solution-based rather than problem-based approach in order to develop a human-focused gamification solution, with a solution-based mindset .
STEP 4

Design Refinement

Refining a chosen direction and polising
visual design, wireframing, prototyping
and documenting the thought process.
STEP 5

Handoff

Collaborating with the developers and working closely with the relevant parties for reviewing the design and leave their feedback . Iterating and fixing the design in accordance with the feedback received
